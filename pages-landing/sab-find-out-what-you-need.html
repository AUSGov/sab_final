2. Find out what you need

Finding out what you need to start your business before diving in can help you plan better and save you from any surprises later on.

Starting a business guide Required

How to manage your suppliers

Are you selling products and need a supplier? See what you should consider when looking for a quality supplier and where you can search for them.

Starting a business guide Required

Register your business the right way

Make sure you have the right registrations and licences for your business. The registrations and licences you need for your business will vary depending on which industry you’re in and the state or territory your business is located in.

Starting a business guide Optional

Hiring employees

You can’t always run a business on your own. If you’re planning on hiring, make sure you know the steps you need to take and your legal responsibilities.
