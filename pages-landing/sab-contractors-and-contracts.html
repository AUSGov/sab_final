4. Contractors and contracts

Contracts are a vital part of doing business. Take the time to make sure you have them right from the beginning.

Starting a business guide Required

Types of contracts

A contract can be anything from a formal written document to a verbal promise. Learn about written, verbal, standard form and period contracts, and things to be aware of before you make an agreement.

Starting a business guide Optional

Employee or contractor?

If you’re working on your own you’ll need to know your worker status. Find out if you’re an independent contractor or an employee first before entering into a contract.
