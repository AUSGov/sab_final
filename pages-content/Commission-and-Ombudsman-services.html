There are a range of support services available from Commissioners and Ombudsman. Some provide support and information specifically for small businesses, while others for business operators.

Support for small business

  • Australian Small Business and Family Enterprise Ombudsman (ASBFEO) - provides information and assistance to small businesses, including referral to dispute resolution services.
  • Fair Work Ombudsman (FWO) - provides information and advice about Australia's workplace rights and rules.
  • Office of the NSW Small Business Commissioner - assists and advocates for small business in New South Wales.
  • Small Business Commissioner South Australia - provides information and advice to help small businesses in South Australia resolve and manage disputes.
  • Victorian Small Business Commissioner - supports Victorian small business and provides independent advice on business disputes.
  • Small Business Development Corporation - provides free advice for small businesses in Western Australia.
